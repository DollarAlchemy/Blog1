Breaking Free from the Default Path

Rediscover your authentic self by escaping societal norms.

Why It Matters:

Societal expectations often push people into predefined scripts—career paths, social roles, and habits that may not align with their true selves. Escaping this cycle is key to living an authentic life.

Key Insights:

  • Growth Mindset: Viewing abilities as developable fosters continuous learning and helps individuals step outside societal norms.
  • Self-Determination Theory: Autonomy, competence, and relatedness are critical for intrinsic motivation.

Actionable Steps:

  1. Define Your “Why”: Write down your core values and revisit this exercise quarterly.
  2. Reframe Failures: Adopt a mindset where setbacks are seen as lessons for growth.
  3. Detach from External Validation: Focus on intrinsic rewards over societal markers of success.
