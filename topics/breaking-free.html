Breaking Free from the Default Path

Challenge societal norms and build an authentic life.

Why It Matters:

Living authentically means stepping away from societal scripts and creating a life aligned with your values and purpose.

Key Insights:

  • Growth Mindset: Embrace continuous learning and development.
  • Intrinsic Motivation: Focus on autonomy and competence over external validation.

Actionable Steps:

  1. Define Your “Why”: Identify core values and set goals that align with them.
  2. Reframe Failures: Keep a “failure journal” to extract lessons.
  3. Detach from Validation: Focus on intrinsic rewards rather than societal expectations.
